Question: How did the identity sharing go? What did you learn about yourself? What will it mean in terms of overcoming your personal values as you seek to learn new things? How will that same identity contribute to helping you push theory and research? Where do you think one's identity really comes from? How much of an impact does it have on teaching and learning? Why? What examples can you give? Explain.

 

Response: Identity plays a significant role in people’s lives. When it comes to identity, it can be broad and narrow to me. From a broad perspective, identity is the condition of a person. On the other hand, it also represents a person’s characters. In my opinion, most aspects of a person’s identity come after his or her experiences, which are influenced by their family background. People survive in society, so everyone has their own identity and responsibilities such as a person who is another person’s child, so he or she needs to take care of their parents. People can establish their character from learning and experiences. Many stimulations in the environment are powerful to influence people’s conditions which is an important part of a person’s identity.

 

Since everyone comes from different backgrounds and own distinct identities, every student has their own learning preferences and styles. It is complex for teachers to prepare classes with a balance to suit every individual’s needs. To the best of my knowledge, I believe people can learn new knowledge, skills, and attitudes by themselves because they know what they want to learn and how they will like to deal with their learning processes. Teachers are like students’ mentors and coaches to guide and help students address their problems.

 

Everyone has different preferences and interests, so they will have a different research topic. Based on my own experience, it is not easy to find out what I really like to conduct a research. I consider that I need time to search for a topic that is narrow enough for my research. However, personality which is part of a person’s identity will make an impact on research. The way people make a plan is totally different from others. Even if they have the same picture, they will progress in different procedures.

 

Education plays a significant role in people’s lives. Almost everyone had experiences of being a student because learning is happening everywhere and anytime in people’s lives. On the other hand, not everyone had teaching experiences which are as much as the experiences of being students. We cannot ignore that a person’s hobby and habit can make people accomplish a thing in different way, so there are many kinds of teachers and students. One teacher cannot be liked by every student, but teachers try their best to suit every students’ needs and preferences. Although it is unfair, I think it is the teachers’ duty to help every student.
